> About Me

Hi, I’m Mubashir Jilani, a passionate frontend developer, graphic designer, and freelancer. I specialize in creating responsive and visually appealing websites. I completed my web development studies from SMIT, and I have been working in the field for over a year. During this time, I’ve been focused on improving my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Adobe Illustrator, and WordPress.

As a frontend developer, I am deeply committed to creating seamless, user-friendly experiences. I take pride in writing clean, maintainable code that meets the latest web standards. My focus is on building responsive websites that adapt well across devices, ensuring optimal performance and design. I’m also keen on optimizing websites for speed and accessibility, ensuring that users have the best possible experience regardless of their device or environment.

I’m always eager to learn new technologies, improve my skills, and stay up-to-date with the latest trends in web development and design. In my free time, I love exploring new design tools and keeping my creative energy flowing.
